A search engine for Gregory Maskarinec's images

Gregory Maskarinec took numerous photos during his research on shamanism in Nepal.

This website provides a means to "slice-and-dice" some of the myriad data elements of the "Gregory Maskarinec Image Archive". The material available here is only part of the collection. It consists of 51,854 images (scanned from 35mm slides), organized by Maskarinec by date, name, location, and "Locke Number". (Locke, John K. Buddhist Monasteries of Nepal: A Survey of the Bāhās and Bahīs of the Kathmandu Valley)

The material is organized into three "Collections":

  • Bāhās and Bahīs..., curated images of Buddhist temples in the Kathmandu Valley.
  • Nepal Photo Archive..., a larger collection of images taken in Nepal.
  • Toranas..., selected toranas.

This is a work in progress! Caveat utilizator!

It is a search engine that allows you to search for 5 types of Gregory Maskarinec data:

  • "Collection" (Maskarinec organized materials into a folder hierarchy; the top level has 3 divisions, and has been rendered as a searchable facet on the left)
  • Site (Bāhā or Bahī name)
  • Locke number
  • City (location of site)
  • Year (photo was taken)

So, in this case, the result is always:

  • Images

Besides these images, the collection includes hundreds of documents and dozens of video and audio recordings.
